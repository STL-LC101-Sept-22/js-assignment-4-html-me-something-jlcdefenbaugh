Pregnant Mother of Four Finally Told How Babies Are Made

Pays Stranger $30 For Life Lesson

By Jami Defenbaugh

ST. LOUIS, MO—Local man Bob Tate became a hero Saturday morning thanks to his sharp eye and keen insight.

Tate, 63, said he’d just purchased some frozen dinners and was headed across the Target parking lot when he saw a pregnant woman with a large brood and felt compelled to openly question her understanding of contraception.

“No truly informed person has that many kids,” Tate said. “Of course I asked her if she knew what caused ‘em. It’s what you do. You look out for your neighbor.”

Witnesses said the mother appeared to cycle through feelings of confusion, shock, denial, and even fury, but eventually chocked out thanks in between sobs of joy.

It didn’t take long for a crowd to form.

“We were all just so moved. I guess no one had ever bothered showing her that sort of kindness before,” said Vivian Kirk, who said she watched the entire exchange while her husband, Hank, unloaded their cart. “She insisted on giving him money, and then she went and asked him what he thought about the size of her body.”

“That part was a little weird, if you ask me,” Vivian added.

“I know,” Hank said. “She said she was only 22 weeks. Is there more than one in there or something?”

Tate, who signed autographs well into the evening, said he never could have imagined the outpouring of love and appreciation that followed.

“Shoppers were clapping and saying what a great service I’d done the community. They said they never heard such a question, either, and that they couldn’t believe how clever I was,” he said.

Tate, though, credits a higher power.

“Applause comes and goes, but that cash bought me two weeks of salisbury steak,” he said. “You just never know how the Lord’s gonna bless you.”
