Pregnant Mother of Four Finally Told How Babies Are Made

Pays Stranger $30 For Life Lesson

By Jami Defenbaugh

ST. LOUIS, MO—Local man Bob Tate became a hero Saturday morning thanks to his sharp eye and keen insight.

Tate, 63, said he’d just purchased some frozen dinners and was headed across the Target parking lot when he saw a pregnant woman with a large brood and felt compelled to openly question her understanding of contraception.

“No truly informed person has that many kids,” Tate said. “Of course I asked her if she knew what caused ‘em. It’s what you do. You look out for your neighbor.”

Witnesses said the mother appeared to cycle through feelings of confusion, shock, denial, and even fury, but eventually chocked out thanks in between sobs of joy.

It didn’t take long for a crowd to form.
